StormBoys Sticker Privacy Policy

I, Wes Johnson, do not collect personally identifying information through my iMessage sticker pack.

Apple collects some non-personal data about users of sticker packs, such as crash data, number of installations per geographic region, and iOS version/Apple devices used with the sticker pack app. See http://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ for more information on what is collected. This information is available to me, Wes Johnson. However I, Wes Johnson, cannot identify users through this information and do not share this information at all.
